What is DSC?

The Developer Student Clubs (DSC) program is a grassroots channel through which we provide development skills for students, towards employability. DSC focuses on conducting workshops that enhance knowledge about developer technologies and bring in collaborative learning. In addition to workshops, we also provide an opportunity for students to apply their newly gained skills to develop solutions for local organizations, and then provide visibility via showcases.

Why is DSC important?

DSC currently is active in 172 colleges in India and more than 100 campuses in Indonesia, Africa .

DSC provides the students get an opportunity to apply their skills for a live problem sourced from the local community (sourced by the DSC community). In addition to development skills, the students gain problem solving skills as they understand more about the problem and are developing a solution using technology, along with communication skills as they learn how to ask questions to better understand the problem. Thus DSC is a great medium through which students can learn developer technologies as well as can help the local community by applying the same knowledge.
